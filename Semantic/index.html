How to talk to a Deaf person

Deaf, not DEAD. People get confused on what Deaf mean - they often think we are dead. No, we are alive and well. Only thing is dead is inside our ears somewhere.


We can do anything but hear.

The Do's and Don't's

  • Don't talk with mouth full of food - we do not want to know what you are eating.
  • Don't scream at us - it does not help us understand you any better.
  • Do speak a bit slowly - we cannot read lips if you are speaking 100 mph.
  • Don't over enunciate - you look funny when you do that.
  • Do repeat what you said - sometimes we misunderstood what you said for example we may be offended if you said 42.
  • Do trim your mustache - it is a little difficult to read lips if your lips are covered.

Words or phrases are considered swear words to the deaf:

  • never mind
  • tell you later
  • not important
  • they are stupid - not important
